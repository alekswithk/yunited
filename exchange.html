Exchange students

Whether you're arriving at HSG for a semester or heading abroad yourself — here's how Yunited fits into your exchange.

Coming to HSG

Incoming exchange students

Spending a semester in St. Gallen? You're warmly invited — no Balkan roots required.

  • Buddy-system: our buddy-system offers guidance for assessment and exchange students — a friendly first contact for questions about HSG and life in St. Gallen.
  • Events: join our brunches, barbecues, karaoke and party nights from week one. Membership (via uniclubs) gives access to all events.
  • A taste of home: if your roots are in the region, we're a way to stay connected to the culture while abroad. If not, we're a fun way to discover it.
