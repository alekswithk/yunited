About Yunited

Fostering cultural exchange between students from former Yugoslavia at the University of St. Gallen — since 2013.

Our mission

What we do

Yunited connects students with roots from former Yugoslavia socially and professionally while fostering cultural exchange at the University of St. Gallen.

We host a variety of social and professional events for students from ex-Yugoslav countries to aid in their personal and professional development during and after their studies at HSG — and everyone interested in the culture is welcome, whether or not your roots are in the region.

Beyond campus, Yunited builds a network between students, supports their academic development, acts as a bridge to companies and organisations with an ex-Yugoslav background, and supports and promotes fundraising campaigns.

Our buddy-system offers guidance for assessment and exchange students while fostering connections across all majors and semesters.

Since 2013

Our story

Yunited was founded in 2013 as the cultural club for students from the ex-Yugoslav region at HSG.

Values

What we stand for

Community

A home away from home: friendships across all majors and semesters, from the Assessment year to Master's and beyond.

Culture

Keeping the food, music, humour and traditions of the region alive in St. Gallen — and sharing them with everyone who's curious.

Careers

Professional events and a bridge to companies and organisations with ex-Yugoslav roots, during your studies and after them.
